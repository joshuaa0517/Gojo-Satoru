About Gojo Satoru

Gojo Satoru (五ご条じょう悟さとる Gojō Satoru) is one of the main protagonists of the Jujutsu Kaisen series. He is a special grade jujutsu sorcerer and recognized as the strongest in the world. Gojo Satoru is the pride of the Gojo Family and the first person to inherit both the Limitless and the Six Eyes. Before being trapped, he worked as a teacher at the Tokyo Jujutsu High while protecting and training strong young allies.
